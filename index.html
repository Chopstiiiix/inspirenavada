04

Between the deadlines

Hackathons are the heartbeat, not the whole body. Most of what happens here happens between them.

a.

Research circles

Small standing groups reading papers, reproducing results and publishing notes together. Two circles have co-authored preprints this year.

b.

Build nights

Tuesdays & Saturdays, open voice rooms, cameras optional. Bring the thing you're stuck on; someone in the room has been stuck there before.

c.

Review exchange

Trade code reviews with builders one tier above you. Reviewing well earns rank points — so the feedback is actually good.

d.

Team finder

Filter by timezone, stack and rank, then form a team in two clicks. 71% of podium teams last season met right here.

The ranking system

Five tiers, one currency. Every scored submission, honest review and published write-up earns points that accumulate for as long as your account exists. Nothing decays, nothing resets, nothing is for sale.

  1. Tier I · 0 pts

    Novice

    Sign up, enter anything. Everyone starts here, including every current Luminary.

    8,102 builders

  2. Tier II · 150 pts

    Contributor

    3 scored entries and 1 published write-up. Proof you finish things.

    4,317 builders

  3. Tier III · 600 pts

    Artisan

    A top-40% finish and 10 accepted peer reviews. Proof you finish things well.

    1,466 builders

  4. Tier IV · 2,000 pts

    Architect

    A podium finish and a hackathon where you led the team. Others build on your judgment.

    289 builders

  5. Tier V · 6,000 pts

    Luminary

    Three podiums and published research. The tier recruiters ask us about by name.

    34 builders

03

What rank gets you

Beyond the number next to your name.

a.

Review weight

Higher tiers carry more weight in peer review — and their reviews of your work carry more signal for you.

b.

Early entry

Architects and Luminaries register for capped events 48 hours before general entry opens.

c.

Circle priority

Research circles with limited seats admit by rank and application. Tier III unlocks founding your own.

d.

A verifiable record

Your profile is a public, timestamped history of shipped work. Recruiters check it; we make sure it can't be faked.
